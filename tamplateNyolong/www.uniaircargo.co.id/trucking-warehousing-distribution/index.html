Uniair Cargo provides reliable inland transportation (trucking) across Indonesia as well as Asia Pacific. Whether you require a full truckloads (FTL) or less-than truckloads (LTL), we can arrange first mile to last mile delivery. We also have more than 50.000 m² of warehouse spaces across Indonesian big cities ready to serve your businesses fulfillment, stock inventory, and many more.
