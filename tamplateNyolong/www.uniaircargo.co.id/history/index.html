History

A Journey Of Our Story

PT Uniair Indotama Cargo (Uniair Cargo) was established in Jakarta in the year
1989 as a freight forwarder company to facilitate logistics and supply chain
operation for exporters and importers in Indonesia.

Get to Know Us Closer

In 1990, Dimerco Express Taiwan, one of Asia's biggest freight forwarding companies, partnered up with PT Uniair Indotama Cargo with the aim to expand our core business and support supply chain operation in Asia Pacific as well as the whole world.

Vision

To be the most competitive global transportation and logistics service provider, integrator, and consultant.

Mission

  • Progressively expand our service network on a global scale.
  • Assist clients in increasing their competitiveness in the market by leveraging external data and information visibility on global supply chain management.

Our Values

Our Milestone

Our Milestone

1989

The establishment of Uniair Cargo, based in Jakarta.

1990

Joint venture with Dimerco Express Group

1997

Strengthening global logistics position by obtaining an ISO certification.

2004

Registered as a member of International Air Transport Association (IATA)

2009

Become a member of GAFEKSI (Gabungan Forwarder dan Ekspedisi Seluruh Indonesia)

2017

Obtained TAPA certification from BUREAU VERITAS.

2018

Obtained AEO certification from Directorate General of Customs and Excise.

2019

1st PLB eCommerce in Indonesia located at Marunda Industrial & Warehouse Estate

2020

Established eCommerce and fulfillment business unit.
