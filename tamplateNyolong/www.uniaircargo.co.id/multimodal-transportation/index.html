Multimodal Transportation Services

Sea-Air and Air-Sea services, This is the ideal service to achieve fast delivery, lower costs and a smaller carbon footprint. Using multiple modes does add some complexity, so it’s important to work with an experienced forwarder like Uniair Cargo. We have enormous experiences coordinating the en route handoffs. We synchronize the two transport modes to ensure you keep your delivery promises to customers.

Multimodal Transport Intra-Asia is one of Uniair Cargo’s main forte. Our team of expert professionals will look for the time and budget limit and get creative with a mix of air, ocean, rail, and road depending on your business needs.

Uniair Cargo in Action


Tech company avoid costly air freight with Sea-Air solution from Indonesia

A Taiwanese tech company needed to get their cargo which was manufactured in Batam to Los Angeles within a short deadline, but direct air freight to Singapore International Airport for flight transfer would be costly.

By putting ourselves in consideration to our client's situation, we proposed an alternate sea-air solution which helped to save cost while maintaining acceptable delivery time. A short ocean journey to Singapore's port and inland transfer to the airport for direct flight to Los Angeles.
